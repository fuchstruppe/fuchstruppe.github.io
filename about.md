FuchsTruppe - Community Orchestra, the place where musicians of all backgrounds and skill levels come together to cultivate a shared passion for music and build lasting connections. Our community orchestra is dedicated to provide a safe and inclusive environment where individuals can express themselves through music and find companionship with fellow members. Our orchestra embraces a diverse repertoire, ranging from classical masterpieces to soundtracks and jazz standards. Providing members with opportunities to explore new genres, techniques and expand their musical horizons.

## Our Mission

At FuchsTruppe, our mission is to bring people together through the language of music. We aim to foster a sense of belonging and community by creating opportunities for individuals to come together, share their musical talents, and forge meaningful relationships in a supportive and nurturing environment.


## Our Motto

The motto of our projects is 'La vida es un Carnaval' which means that Life is a Carnival, and we have to live it by enjoying every moment. And most important: no one's alone, we are a community.
